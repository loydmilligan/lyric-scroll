## The Ask

Add an "Agent Tasks" panel to GC dashboard where humans can:
- See pending tasks from agents
- Click Approve/Reject
- View recently completed tasks

## MVP (Minimum Viable)

1. Subscribe to `agent-sync/tasks/pending` in backend
2. Show pending tasks in a list
3. Approve button publishes status back
